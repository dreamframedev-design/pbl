This high-sensitivity ELISA detects IL-15 and IL-15/IL-15Rα complexes in TCM, serum, and plasma. Calibrated to IL-15, it quantifies levels as low as 0.51 pg/ml in healthy donor matrices.

This kit quantitates _free_ human IL-15 and _bound_ IL-15/IL-15R in serum, plasma and TCM using a sandwich immunoassay. The kit is based on an ELISA with biotinylated-detection antibody and streptavidin-conjugated horseradish peroxidase (HRP). Tetramethyl-benzidine (TMB) is the substrate.
