3.  Human IL-22 ELISA, High Sensitivity (Serum, Plasma, TCM)

Human Interleukin 22 ELISA designed for accurate quantitation of **endogenous human IL-22** in healthy & disease sera/plasma, and tissue culture media samples with an **LLOQ of 0.78 pg/ml**. This assay measures unbound (free) IL-22. IL-22 Binding Protein (IL-22BP) may interfere with the assay if present at high concentrations.
